The data is collected solely for the following purposes:

Data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above. We may periodically purge older data to reduce storage and improve performance.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose user data to any third parties, except as required by law or legal request.

5. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

6. Your Rights

Users may request to view or delete their data by contacting the owner of the Service. We will process such requests within a reasonable timeframe.
